My Favourite Character

Toothless

Toothless is a dragon from the movie and book "How To Train Your Dragon" by Cressida Cowell. He is called toothless because it look as if he has no teeth but when he eats they suddenly appear. He is owned by Hiccup Haddock, a young Viking boy who is fearless but shy at times. Toothless is also the only Nightfury found in history. He was discovered by Hiccup. Toothless appears as a Night Fury in the franchise, the rarest, fastest and most intelligent species of Dragon there is. He is also the only known member of his species to date. He plays a similar role to his book counterpart.

My Insperation

This is Cressida Cowell, The Author of How To Train Your Dragon.

Cressida Cowell MBE (born 15 April 1966 ) is an English children's author, popularly known for the book series, How to Train Your Dragon, which has subsequently become an award-winning franchise as adapted for the screen by DreamWorks Animation. As of 2015, the series has sold more than seven million copies around the world. In addition to her other publications, Cowell works with illustrator Neal Layton in the ongoing series of Emily Brown stories. The first in the series, That Rabbit Belongs to Emily Brown, won a Nestlé Children’s Book Award.
